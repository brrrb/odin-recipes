Ingredients

Caramel

  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup water

Creme

  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 full eggs
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup of evaporated milk

Steps

  1. For the caramel, mix ¼ water and ½ cup sugar till sugar is dissolved
  2. Put that on the stove on high and do not stir. Not stirring ensures everything boils evenly.
  3. Wait until it's past bubbling and a light brown golden colour before taking off the stove
  4. Cover bottoms of the ramekins/containers quickly before the caramel hardens

    5. Tip! The boiling happens in a matter of minutes, caramel burns quickly, so it's advised you don't leave the stove or room until you're done.

  5. Beat your eggs and egg yolks together till emulsified Additionally you can see foam
  6. Incorporate ¼ cup sugar slowly while whisking
  7. Mix in your water and evaporated milk
  8. Optional (but for a better aesthetic), strain out the foam bubbles
  9. Pour into containers and set the containers on a tray with 1-2cm water bath
  10. Bake for 1h at 325 ℉. If you have very small containers, do less time like 55 minutes
  11. Let it sit in the fridge for at least a few hours, ideally overnight.

Notes: The amount of creme caramel you get will depend on how big your eggs are. It's variable. Additionally, this fill ~4 mini ramekins
