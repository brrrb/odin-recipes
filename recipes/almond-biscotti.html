Almond Biscotti

Description

This recipe is from my grandmother who loves to bake. This is the thing she bakes the most for us to take home during long road trips. The image is ripped off of the internet.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup almonds toasted, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp almond essence
  • ⅔ cup sugar
  • 1 ½ cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 ℉
  2. Beat eggs till fluffy
  3. Add sugar and almond extract and beat till thick + lemon colour
  4. Fold in sifted flour, mixed with salt and baking powder
  5. Fold in chopped almonds
  6. Use 2 cookie sheets. Grease the centre of each sheet strip 10" by 4" and spoon ½ the almond mixture on each sheet
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
  8. Remove from oven and cool
  9. Leave oven on
  10. Slice each log diagonally with a serrated knife (makes about 12 slices)
  11. Place each slice flat on sheet and bake for another 5 minutes at 350
  12. Turn over slice and bake for 5-7 minutes more till the colour is golden
  13. Cool completely before storin in airtight container
